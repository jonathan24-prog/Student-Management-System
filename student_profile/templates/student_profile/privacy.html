Privacy Protection for Student Profiles

BCConnect provides options for students to manage and control the visibility of their personal information. Your privacy is a priority, and we are committed to ensuring that your profile data remains secure. Here’s how BCConnect protects your privacy:

1. Customizable Profile Settings

  • Students can control which personal details are visible, such as name, profile photo, contact information, or academic achievements.
  • Only the information you choose to share will be visible to others within the system.

2. Restricted Access

  • Your profile information is accessible only to authorized users within BCC, including faculty, staff, and authorized parents or guardians.
  • External parties do not have access to student profiles unless explicitly authorized.

3. Data Encryption

  • All information shared on your profile is securely encrypted to prevent unauthorized access.
  • BCConnect uses advanced encryption protocols to protect your data at all times.

4. Consent-Based Sharing

  • Any sharing of information beyond your chosen settings on BCConnect requires your explicit consent.
  • You may update or restrict your information at any time through the profile settings in BCConnect.

5. Regular Security Monitoring

  • BCConnect is continuously monitored to prevent unauthorized access and detect potential security threats.
  • In case of any suspicious activity, immediate steps will be taken to secure user data.

With BCConnect, you maintain control over your profile’s privacy, allowing you to share information with confidence. BCC is committed to providing a secure environment that supports your educational experience.
